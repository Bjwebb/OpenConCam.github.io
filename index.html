Why OpenConCam OpenCon Cambridge?

We initially organaised OpenCon Cambridge, an OpenCon satellite meeting, because we are passionate about open access, open data and open education and we wanted to:

  • Introduce open in all its many forms
  • Bring people together who want to change things in research, education, public data, and much more.
  • Make connections and networks within and outside the University
  • Take forward actions in Cambridge e.g. groups who want to focus on a certain thing, changes we can ask for internally, training or research that people might be interested in pursuing.

This was a great experience, which prompted us to continue our activities throughout the year, an started the OpenConCam group.
